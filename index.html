Diet Impacts Learning

In 2008, a Journal of School Health study discovered that literary assessment scores were negatively affected by fast food. A follow-up study confirmed that fast food reduced the reading skill of elementary school children.

Direct Effects

Nutritional deficiencies have been proven to negatively affect cognitive development. There is also evidence that diets heavy with trans and saturated fats can have a negative impact on cognition.
